How it works

One key. Three steps.

  1. 01

    Hold fn

    Recording begins the moment you press the key. There is no window to open and no application to switch to — a small capsule appears to indicate that recording is in progress.

  2. 02

    Speak

    Speak naturally, as you would in conversation. Pauses, corrections, and mid-sentence restarts are handled by the Whisper model.

  3. 03

    Release

    Your words are transcribed and inserted at the cursor almost immediately, and the previous contents of your clipboard are automatically restored.

Features

Designed to integrate
seamlessly with your Mac.

Private by default

Transcription runs entirely on your Mac, using local Whisper models accelerated by the Metal GPU. Your voice is never uploaded and never stored outside your control — Linty works fully offline.

Optional cloud transcription

Enable a single setting to transcribe with Groq when you need long recordings returned in under a second.

Inserts text at your cursor

No manual copying and pasting. Text is placed exactly where you were typing, and your clipboard contents are preserved and restored.

Works in every application

Slack, Mail, your code editor, your CRM — if an application accepts text input, Linty can type into it.

Resides in the menu bar

No Dock icon and no clutter. A small capsule appears while you speak and disappears when you finish.

A complete transcription history

Every transcription is saved to a local history — search it, copy it again, or clear it at any time. Your data remains on your disk, stored as plain JSON.

Your voice never leaves your Mac.

Local Whisper runs on Apple Silicon. There is no account, no telemetry on your speech, and no audio sent to any server — unless you explicitly enable cloud mode. The code is open source; review it yourself.
